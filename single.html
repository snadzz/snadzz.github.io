A Showcase of some of the work that has kept me busy recently

November 30, 2023 N Dubazana
I actually really enjoy building tools, whether it be on Excel (favorite) or Access or even HTML. I think a soft tool is such a life-enhancing object in the right hand. I Think Microsoft Access is still the best with data-processing for spreadsheets that need manual intervention monthly. I am not happy the world is slowly forgetting about that power house. Yes for queries that require a lot out of a database, when you are constantly exploring new dimensions and measures of data, then without a doubt a SQL query will take you a long way. It also helps to know some machine learning to do a bit of projection with interesting data structures. Combining all these tools and having to work through all the technicalities that be, can make one feel like Neo in the matrix, but the actual thrill of applying basic programming knowledge, with some OOD to a fun project will remind you of why this is the best profession in the world.
